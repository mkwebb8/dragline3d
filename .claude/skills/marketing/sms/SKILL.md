# SMS Marketing

SMS works where immediacy matters. It earns the right to interrupt because of opt-in. Not another email channel — for time-sensitive, high-value messages only.

## Flow Types

- **Welcome**: Immediately after opt-in; deliver the promised incentive
- **Abandoned cart**: 1 hour after abandonment; offer help, not just a discount
- **Post-purchase**: Order confirmation, shipping updates, review request
- **Win-back**: Lapsed customers; urgency + incentive
- **Promotional**: Flash sales, limited offers; use sparingly or unsubscribes spike

## Compliance First

**US Compliance:**
- TCPA: Written consent required before sending marketing SMS
- A2P 10DLC: Register your brand and campaign before sending at scale (carriers require this)
- Double opt-in recommended for marketing lists

**EU/UK Compliance:**
- GDPR: Explicit consent, right to erasure, data residency
- Different rules per country — get legal review

**Universal**: Always include opt-out instructions (STOP to unsubscribe). Honor immediately.

## Copy Rules

- 160 characters = 1 SMS credit. Stay under.
- Lead with the value, not your brand name
- One clear CTA per message
- Include opt-out: "Reply STOP to unsubscribe"
- Personalization: First name minimum

## Measurement

- Delivery rate (>95% is healthy)
- Click-through rate (10–20% benchmark for promotional)
- Conversion rate from click
- Unsubscribe rate (<2% per campaign; >2% = list fatigue or irrelevant messaging)

## Integration

- emails: SMS + email as a coordinated retention system
- analytics: attribution and conversion tracking
- churn-prevention: win-back sequences
