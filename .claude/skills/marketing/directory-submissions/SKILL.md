# Directory Submissions

Build a compounding backlink and discovery foundation through strategic directory submissions. Directories pass dofollow backlinks, create discovery surface area, and get cited by AI engines (ChatGPT, Claude, Perplexity pull heavily from high-DR directories).

## Three Hard Rules

**Rule 1: Foundation before submission**
Never submit until live: single H1, sequential headings, pricing page, privacy/terms, logo assets, 5–8 real screenshots, 60–90s demo video, FAQ schema, structured data.

**Rule 2: Destination pages before directories**
You need pages for link equity to land on:
- 3–5 competitor alternative pages (convert at 5–15% vs. 0.5–2% generic)
- 3–5 use-case pages
- Template gallery (if applicable)
- One honest "best of" blog post

**Rule 3: Positioning varies by directory type**
Never copy-paste descriptions:
- Startup directories: lead with **outcome**
- SaaS directories: lead with **alternative framing** ("The [X] alternative that...")
- AI directories: lead with **AI-first architecture**

## Readiness Checklist (9 Questions)

1. Product publicly accessible (no paywall)?
2. Pricing page live?
3. Privacy policy + terms live?
4. Logo assets in PNG + SVG + square + favicon?
5. 5–8 real screenshots + 60–90s demo video?
6. Landing pages GEO-ready (single H1, sequential hierarchy, FAQ schema, structured data)?
7. At least 3 alternative pages and 3 use-case pages live?
8. Template gallery or lead magnet (if applicable)?
9. At least 20 beta users who could review on G2?

"A 'no' on any of 1–7 is a hard block."

## Required Inputs

1. What are you launching?
2. Launch date?
3. Destination pages exist?
4. Product Hunt strategy?
5. Beta users available for reviews?
6. Current domain rating and referring domains?
7. MCP or agent angle?
