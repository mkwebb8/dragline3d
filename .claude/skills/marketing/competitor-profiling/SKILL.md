# Competitor Profiling

Deep competitor research producing structured, source-traceable profiles. Covers positioning, features, pricing, SEO metrics, strengths, and weaknesses.

## What Gets Delivered

- **Individual competitor profiles**: Structured markdown with positioning, features, pricing, SEO metrics, strengths/weaknesses
- **Raw data archive**: Scraped content saved for auditing and future updates
- **Summary comparison**: Cross-competitor overview with positioning map and market gaps

## Research Process

1. **Map each site** — discover key pages (homepage, pricing, features, about)
2. **Extract messaging** — value proposition, target audience, key claims
3. **Document pricing** — all tiers, pricing model, what's included
4. **Pull SEO data** — domain authority, organic traffic estimate, top ranking keywords
5. **Synthesize** — combine into comparable, fact-based profiles
6. **Date and source everything** — profiles are current snapshots; mark the date

## What to Document Per Competitor

- One-liner and headline copy
- Target customer (who they say they serve)
- Key differentiators claimed
- Pricing model and tier structure
- Top-reviewed features (G2/Capterra)
- Common complaints (G2/Capterra negative reviews)
- SEO footprint and content strategy
- Social presence and engagement

## Key Rule

All claims are traceable to sources. No "they say X" without a URL and date.
