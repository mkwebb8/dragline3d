# Referral & Affiliate Programs

Turn customers into a growth engine. Referred customers show 16–25% higher lifetime value and 2–3x higher referral rates themselves.

## Referral vs. Affiliate Programs

**Referral programs**: Existing customers recommend to their network. Best for lower-ticket, self-serve products with natural word-of-mouth appeal.

**Affiliate programs**: Creators and influencers reach their audiences. Better for higher-ticket products justifying ongoing commissions.

## The Referral Loop

Trigger Moment → Share Action → Convert Referred → Reward → (Loop)

**Key insight**: The loop only works if every step is easy. Friction at any point breaks it.

## Incentive Structures

| Type | Description | Best For |
|------|-------------|----------|
| Single-sided | Reward only the referrer | Loyal, motivated advocates |
| Double-sided | Reward referrer + referred | Accelerating conversions |
| Tiered | More referrals = better rewards | Power users and communities |

## Trigger Moments (When to Ask)

Best timing: post-aha moment, after a milestone, immediately after expressing satisfaction. Never: during onboarding, before they've gotten value, or during a support issue.

## Sharing Mechanics

In-product sharing beats email. Make the unique link visible in the dashboard, not buried in settings. One-click copy > "share to email."

## The Referred Customer Experience

Matters as much as incentive structure. Landing page must address: "My friend said you'd give me X. Where is it?"

## Integration

- emails: referral invitation and reward notification sequences
- onboarding: referred users need different onboarding (they already know what the product does)
- analytics: tracking referral attribution and LTV by source
- launch: referral programs amplify launch momentum
