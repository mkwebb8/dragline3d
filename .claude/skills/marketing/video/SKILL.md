# Video Production

Decide what video you need before picking tools. Strategy first; production second.

## Tool Selection by Use Case

| Need | Tool |
|------|------|
| Templated / repeatable content | Hyperframes, Remotion |
| Original AI footage | Veo 3, Sora 2, Runway |
| Talking-head presenter | HeyGen, Synthesia (AI avatars) |
| Manual editing | CapCut, DaVinci Resolve |

## Agent-Native Pipeline

Most automation-friendly: Hyperframes (HTML-based) + HeyGen MCP server + video model APIs = no manual editing steps.

## Platform Formats

| Platform | Aspect Ratio | Max Length |
|----------|-------------|------------|
| YouTube | 16:9 | Unlimited |
| TikTok | 9:16 | 10 min |
| Instagram Reels | 9:16 | 90 sec |
| LinkedIn | 16:9 or 1:1 | 10 min |
| Twitter/X | 16:9 | 2:20 |

## Best Practices

**Always include captions**: 85% of social video is watched without sound.

**Text in video**: AI video models struggle with readable text overlays — use programmatic solutions (Hyperframes, Remotion) for text-heavy video.

**Thumbnails**: Custom thumbnails improve CTR by 30–40% on YouTube.

**First 3 seconds**: On social platforms, viewers decide to keep watching in the first 3 seconds. Hook immediately.
