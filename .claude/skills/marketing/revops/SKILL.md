# Revenue Operations (RevOps)

Design and optimize the revenue engine connecting marketing, sales, and customer success. Fix handoff leaks; build systems that scale.

## Core Areas

- **Lead lifecycle**: Stage definitions, entry/exit criteria, SLAs
- **Lead scoring**: Fit (firmographic) + engagement (behavioral) models with negative scoring
- **Lead routing**: Assignment logic, speed-to-lead, fallback handling
- **Pipeline management**: Stage hygiene, required fields, stale deal detection
- **CRM automation**: Handoff workflows, task creation, SLA alerts
- **Deal desk**: Approval workflows and exception handling
- **Data hygiene**: Deduplication, enrichment, quarterly audits
- **RevOps metrics**: Dashboard design and benchmarking

## Required Context

1. GTM motion (product-led, sales-led, or hybrid)
2. ACV range (drives urgency and process complexity)
3. Current stack (CRM, marketing automation, enrichment)
4. Current pain points (what's breaking in the handoff?)
5. Specific goal (fixing leaks, building from scratch, optimizing conversion)

## Key Metrics

- MQL → SQL conversion rate
- SQL → Closed-Won rate
- Speed-to-lead (minutes from MQL to first contact)
- Pipeline coverage (3x quota is a common target)
- Net Revenue Retention (the ultimate health metric)

## Lead Scoring Model

Fit score (who they are):
- Company size, industry, title = positive or negative points
- Technology stack signals (uses X, doesn't use Y)

Engagement score (what they've done):
- Pricing page visit, demo request, high-intent content = high points
- Unsubscribe, competitor pages = negative points

## Integration

- analytics: data foundation for scoring and attribution
- sales-enablement: assets and content that support the sales motion
- churn-prevention: customer success handoff from sales
- marketing-plan: revenue targets that drive marketing strategy
