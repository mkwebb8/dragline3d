# Prospecting

Build qualified prospect lists. Compliance first: public business channels only, retain source URLs and dates, verify email deliverability.

## Three Branches

| Branch | Sell To | Key Signal |
|--------|---------|-----------|
| **SaaS** | Digital businesses | Tech stack + growth signals (funding, hiring) |
| **B2B** | Non-SaaS enterprises | Industry fit + trigger events |
| **Local SMB** | Neighborhood businesses | Active + proximity + decision-maker access |

## Five-Phase Process

1. **Define ICP**: Firmographic fit, buying signals, decision-maker profiles, disqualifiers
2. **Build candidate list**: Source 2–3× target volume using cross-verified sources
3. **Qualify**: Score each against ICP with evidence and confidence levels
4. **Score and prioritize**: Hot / Warm / Cold / Skip rubric with branch-specific signals
5. **Output**: Markdown table or CSV with top targets highlighted

## Compliance Rules

- Public business channels only (info@, contact@, named-role emails)
- No bulk scraping of LinkedIn, Google Maps, or paywalled sites
- Retain source URLs and dates for every contact (GDPR/CAN-SPAM)
- Verify email deliverability before adding to outreach lists

## Tools by Branch

**SaaS**: Apollo.io, Clay, LinkedIn Sales Navigator, BuiltWith (tech stack)
**B2B**: ZoomInfo, Clearbit, Apollo, industry association directories
**Local SMB**: Google Maps (manual), Yelp, local chamber of commerce

## Required Inputs

1. Which branch (SaaS/B2B/Local SMB)?
2. ICP definition?
3. Target lead count?
4. Available tools?
5. Primary buying signal?
6. Geography?
7. Output format (chat table or CSV)?
