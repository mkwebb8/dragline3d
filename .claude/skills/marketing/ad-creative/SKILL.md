# Ad Creative

Generate and optimize ad copy at scale. Two modes: generate from scratch, or iterate from performance data.

## Mode 1: Generate from Scratch

1. Define 3–5 distinct angles (pain point, outcome, social proof, urgency, curiosity)
2. Generate multiple variations per angle — vary word choice, specificity, tone, structure
3. Validate against platform character limits
4. Organize for upload

## Mode 2: Iterate from Performance Data

Analyze existing campaign metrics to identify:
- **Winning themes** in top-performing creative
- **Winning structures** (questions vs. statements, use of numbers)
- **Patterns in underperformers** to avoid repeating

Then generate fresh variations that build on winners while testing new angles.

## Platform Character Limits

| Platform | Field | Limit |
|----------|-------|-------|
| Google RSA | Headline | 30 chars |
| Google RSA | Description | 90 chars |
| Meta | Primary text (visible) | 125 chars |
| LinkedIn | Intro text (recommended) | 150 chars |
| TikTok | Ad text | 100 chars |

## Quality Standards

- Headlines: specific, benefit-focused, action-oriented
- Descriptions: complement (don't repeat) the headline — add proof points or handle objections
- Flag anything exceeding limits; provide trimmed alternatives

## Integration

- ads: campaign strategy and targeting that creative serves
- image: visual creative to accompany ad copy
- video: video ad scripts and creative briefs
- ab-testing: testing creative variations systematically
