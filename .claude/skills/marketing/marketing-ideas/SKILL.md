# Marketing Ideas

139 proven marketing ideas across 16 categories. Output: 3–5 tailored recommendations with implementation steps, resources needed, timeline, and likely outcomes.

## 16 Categories

1. Content & SEO
2. Paid Ads
3. Social & Community
4. Email
5. Partnerships & Co-marketing
6. Events (virtual and in-person)
7. PR & Media
8. Product Launches
9. Product-Led Growth
10. Developer Marketing
11. Influencer & Creator
12. Unconventional / Viral
13. Directories & Listings
14. Free Tools (Engineering as Marketing)
15. Sales Enablement
16. International / Localization

## Required Context

To recommend relevant ideas:
- Product stage (pre-launch, early, growth, scale)
- Main growth challenge right now
- Budget and team size
- What's already worked (or failed)
- Target customer (B2B vs B2C, company size, etc.)

## Output Format Per Idea

- What it is and why it fits your situation
- Implementation steps (specific, actionable)
- Resources required (time, budget, skills)
- Realistic timeline to first results
- Risk level and common failure modes

## Key Principle

The best marketing idea is the one your team will actually execute well. A mediocre idea executed perfectly beats a brilliant idea executed poorly.

## Integration

- marketing-plan: incorporating ideas into a 12-month strategy
- content-strategy: content and SEO ideas execution
- co-marketing: partnership ideas execution
- free-tools: engineering as marketing implementation
