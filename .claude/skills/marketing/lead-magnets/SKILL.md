# Lead Magnets

Create lead magnets that capture emails and attract high-quality leads. The best lead magnets solve a specific problem in under 30 minutes.

## Lead Magnet Formats

| Format | Best For | Effort |
|--------|----------|--------|
| Checklist | Quick reference, tactical | Low |
| Template | Saving time on a recurring task | Medium |
| Ebook / Guide | Comprehensive reference | High |
| Webinar | Live education and Q&A | Medium |
| Quiz | Personalization, diagnosis | Medium |
| Calculator / Tool | Immediate quantified value | High |
| Resource library | Ongoing reference | High |
| Email course | Sequential education | Medium |

## Gating Strategy

**Gate when**: High perceived value, lead quality matters more than reach, email is essential for delivery.

**Don't gate when**: SEO and organic reach matter more, asset is foundational for trust, audience is skeptical of forms.

**Middle path**: Partial content ungated → gate the full version.

## Required Structure

Every lead magnet needs:
1. A specific promise (not "marketing tips" — "5-step checklist to audit your homepage in 20 minutes")
2. Delivery within 30 minutes of use
3. One actionable next step (leading toward your product)

## Inputs Needed

- Your business and target audience
- Current lead generation state and conversion rates
- Content assets or expertise available to package
- Goal (list growth, lead quality, or education)
- Timeline and resources
