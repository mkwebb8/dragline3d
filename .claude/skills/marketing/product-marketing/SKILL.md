# Product Marketing Context

This is the foundational skill. Create and maintain `.agents/product-marketing.md` — the single source of truth that all other marketing skills read before executing.

## Quick Start

Check first: does `.agents/product-marketing.md` already exist? If yes, read it and ask which sections need updating.

Two paths:
1. **Auto-draft from codebase** (faster): Review README, landing pages, existing copy → create V1 draft for refinement
2. **Start from scratch**: Walk through each section conversationally

## What Gets Captured

- **Product Overview**: One-liner, category, business model
- **Target Audience**: Company type, decision-makers, primary use case, jobs to be done
- **Personas**: (B2B) User, champion, decision-maker perspectives
- **Problems & Pain Points**: Core challenges, why alternatives fail, emotional tension
- **Competitive Landscape**: Direct, secondary, and indirect competitors
- **Differentiation**: Key capabilities and why customers choose you
- **Objections & Anti-Personas**: Sales objections and who's not a fit
- **Switching Dynamics**: Push, pull, habit, and anxiety factors
- **Customer Language**: Verbatim problem/solution descriptions and glossary
- **Brand Voice**: Tone, style, personality
- **Proof Points**: Metrics, customers, testimonials, value themes
- **Goals**: Business objectives and conversion metrics

## Key Principle

Specificity over polish. Exact customer language beats polished copy. Request examples when answers feel vague. Validate each section before moving forward.
