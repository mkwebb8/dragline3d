# Paid Ads

Performance marketing across platforms: Google Ads, Meta, LinkedIn, Twitter/X, TikTok. Strategy, structure, targeting, optimization, and reporting.

## What This Covers

- Campaign strategy, goal-setting, and platform selection
- Account structure, naming conventions, and budget allocation
- Audience targeting and retargeting strategies
- Optimization levers for CPA, CTR, and CPM
- Reporting, tracking setup, and common mistakes
- Google RSA generation with full compliance

## Platform Selection

| Platform | Best For |
|----------|----------|
| Google Search | High-intent demand capture |
| Google Display | Retargeting and awareness |
| Meta | B2C, visual products, broad audiences |
| LinkedIn | B2B, job-title targeting, enterprise |
| TikTok | Gen Z, viral creative, DTC |

## Ad Copy Frameworks

- **PAS**: Problem → Agitate → Solution
- **BAB**: Before → After → Bridge
- **Social Proof**: Specific result → who achieved it → your product enabled it

## Account Structure

Campaign → Ad Set → Ad. Name everything consistently. Structure by objective (awareness/consideration/conversion), then audience.

## Required Before Starting

- Conversion tracking verified and firing
- Clear CPA or ROAS target
- At least 3 creative variants per ad set
- Audience definitions documented

## Integration

- ad-creative: creative production for campaigns
- analytics: conversion tracking setup
- ab-testing: creative and audience experimentation
- marketing-plan: paid channel allocation and budget
