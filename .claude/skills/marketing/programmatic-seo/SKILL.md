# Programmatic SEO

Build SEO-optimized pages at scale using templates and data. The core rule: unique value per page, not variable substitution. Better 100 great pages than 10,000 thin ones.

## 12 Playbook Patterns

1. **Templates & Examples** — Creative products (Notion templates, email templates)
2. **Comparisons** — X vs Y, X alternatives, best X for Y
3. **Conversions** — Unit converters, calculators, format transformers
4. **Locations** — City/region-specific pages (X in New York)
5. **Personas** — Role-specific pages (X for marketers, X for developers)
6. **Integrations** — Product integration pages
7. **Directories** — Curated lists of tools, resources, or providers
8. **Glossaries** — Definition pages for industry terms
9. **Profiles** — Person, company, or product profiles
10. **Use Cases** — Specific workflow or job-to-be-done pages
11. **Reviews** — Aggregated review pages
12. **How-To Guides** — Step-by-step instructional pages at scale

## Key Technical Principles

**URL structure**: Subfolders (`yoursite.com/templates/`) not subdomains — consolidates domain authority.

**Data hierarchy**: Proprietary > licensed > public data for competitive defensibility.

**Quality gates**: Every page needs a unique introduction, not just variable substitution.

## What to Avoid

Doorway pages, keyword stuffing, duplicate content, orphan pages (no internal links), keyword cannibalization between pages.

## Implementation Steps

1. Research repeating keyword patterns and validate search demand
2. Identify data sources and update mechanisms
3. Design templates with unique per-page content elements
4. Build hub-and-spoke internal linking architecture
5. Monitor indexation, rankings, traffic, and engagement post-launch
