# Content Strategy

Plan content that is searchable, shareable, or both. Searchable content captures existing demand; shareable content creates demand through novel insights or counterintuitive perspectives. Build search traffic as the foundation.

## Pre-Planning Context

Gather before starting:
- Business goals and conversion metrics
- Customer research and pain points
- Competitive content landscape
- Available resources (writers, budget, publishing cadence)

## Content Types

- **Use-case articles**: Specific problem → specific solution
- **Hub-and-spoke**: Pillar page + cluster of supporting posts
- **Thought leadership**: Novel insight or counterintuitive take
- **Data-driven**: Original research or proprietary data
- **Comparison/alternative**: Competitor comparison pages

## Content Pillars

Choose 3–5 core topics that spawn related subtopic clusters. Pillars organize strategy and build topical authority systematically.

## Buyer Journey Alignment

Map content to funnel stage using keyword modifiers:
- **Awareness**: "what is X", "how does X work", "X problems"
- **Consideration**: "best X tools", "X alternatives", "X comparison"
- **Decision**: "X pricing", "X review", "X vs Y"
- **Implementation**: "how to use X", "X tutorial", "X setup"

## Ideation Sources

Keyword research, customer call transcripts, support tickets, surveys, forum discussions (Reddit, Quora), competitor content gaps, sales team input.

## Prioritization Framework

Score ideas on:
- Customer impact (40%)
- Content-market fit (30%)
- Search potential (20%)
- Resource requirements (10%)
