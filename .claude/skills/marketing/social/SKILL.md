# Social Media

Organic social content strategy and creation across platforms. Build sustainable systems, not one-off posts.

## Pre-Content Planning

- Goals and success metrics
- Target audience and platform fit
- Brand voice and personality
- Available resources (time, design, video)

## Platform-Specific Strategies

| Platform | Content Type | Posting Frequency |
|----------|-------------|-------------------|
| LinkedIn | Thought leadership, case studies | 3–5x/week |
| Twitter/X | Takes, threads, engagement | 1–3x/day |
| Instagram | Visual, behind-scenes, Reels | 4–7x/week |
| TikTok | Short-form video, trends | Daily |
| YouTube | Long-form, tutorials | 1–2x/week |

## Content Pillars Framework

Choose 3–5 pillars that define what you consistently post about. Each pillar spawns multiple content formats.

## Hook Formulas

- "The [counterintuitive truth] about [topic]"
- "Most [audience] [common mistake]. Here's what to do instead:"
- "[Number] things I wish I knew before [experience]"
- "Unpopular opinion: [take]"

## Content Atoms (Repurposing)

Extract self-contained moments from long-form content (podcasts, blog posts, webinars) for multi-platform use. One recording → 10+ pieces.

## Short-Form Video (TikTok/Reels/Shorts)

The 3-second rule: simultaneous visual hook + verbal hook + text hook within the first second. Always include captions (85% watch without sound).
