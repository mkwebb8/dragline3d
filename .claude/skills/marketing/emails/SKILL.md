# Email Sequences

Design email sequences that nurture relationships and drive conversions. One job per email. Value before ask. Relevance over volume.

## Sequence Types

- **Welcome sequences**: Post-signup activation (3–7 emails)
- **Lead nurture flows**: Pre-sale trust-building (5–12 emails)
- **Re-engagement campaigns**: Win-back for inactive subscribers
- **Onboarding sequences**: Product user activation
- **Lifecycle programs**: Automated trigger-based workflows

## Required Inputs

1. What type of sequence?
2. What triggers entry?
3. Primary goal (signup, purchase, upgrade, activation)?
4. Who is the audience and what do they already know?
5. How long is your typical sales cycle?

## Core Principles

**One job per email**: Each email has one purpose and one CTA. Not two.

**Value before ask**: Deliver something useful before requesting action.

**Strategic timing**: Match send cadence to the decision timeline. B2B decisions take weeks; don't compress to 3 days.

**Relevance over volume**: A 5-email sequence where every email matters outperforms a 12-email sequence with filler.

## Platform Integrations

Mailchimp, Customer.io, Klaviyo, SendGrid, Iterable — sequence logic is platform-agnostic; implementation varies.

## Integration

- onboarding: post-signup activation (complements email sequences)
- cold-email: outbound prospecting sequences
- churn-prevention: save and win-back sequences
- analytics: measuring open rates, click rates, and downstream conversions
